# 📖 About the Book

## 🖋️ Why I wrote this book

After working in corporate front-end development for many years, I started my journey as an independent developer. I soon realized there was a gap between the internal tools I was used to and the technologies used in the wider market. Without a company’s infrastructure, I had to relearn how to develop and deploy projects on my own.

This book is a way to document my process of learning and practicing. I decided to organize my notes and findings into this manual to share what I’ve learned during this transition.

---

## ✨ Features

* **Real** 🛠️
    Based on my personal learning experiences and the challenges I actually faced.
* **Practical** 🚀
    Focused on what works. I prioritize methods and tools that help build and launch projects effectively.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

* **Beginners** 🌱
    If you feel overwhelmed by the many terms and technologies out there, you can use this as a reference for a potential learning path.
* **Experienced Developers** 👨‍💻
    If you are looking for specific materials or want to see how someone else handles independent development.
